Transgaming subscription

A subscription to TransGaming's services opens at least two possibilities to users:

  • They are allowed to download a beefed up version of the Open Source product Cedega, allowing them to run certain games under LINUX.
  • They are allowed to vote on aspects of the product that they want to see improved in future releases. The number of votes a customer has depends on the amount of money he's willing to pay for his subscription.
