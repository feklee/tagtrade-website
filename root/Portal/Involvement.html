Involvement

There are several ways to get involved in the evolution of tag trade:

  • Let other's know what you consider important, and voice your ideas. The following resources are targeted at this purpose:
    • The main wiki: Everything about tag trade, including specifications.
    • The development wiki: Everything concerning development of the site TagTrade.org, including the portal and the tag database.
    • Discussion groups: News groups for discussion.
    • tagtrade@conference.jabber.org: Jabber group chat.
    • #tagtrade: An IRC channel on freenode.net.
  • Tag trade: Simply try it out (once there is a usable specification). It's important to apply trade systems in order to see where improvement is necessary.
  • Let others know about tag trade, e.g. by putting a button linking to TagTrade.org on a web page.
