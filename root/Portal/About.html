About

The purpose of the TagTrade portal is to make it easy for new and advanced users to engage in tag based trading on the Internet:

  • For general information and specifications concerning tag based trading, see the Main Wiki Group.
  • For discussion concerning the development of the portal, see the Development Wiki Group.
  • For playing with Wiki markup, use the portal's Sandbox.

The goal of the portal is not to be the best portal to tag based trading on the internet. The goal is to show how such a portal could work. So, it should be perceived as an experiment.

Finally: Feel free to improve and extend the TagTrade portal - it's a Wiki after all!
